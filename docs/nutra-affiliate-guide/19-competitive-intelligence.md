### Major Advertisers / Product Owners

```
TIER 1 — LARGE ADVERTISERS
═══════════════════════════

Advertisers trong nutra thường private companies, ít public info.
Cách identify:

1. Xem offer sources trên các network lớn
2. Check landing pages → tìm company info footer
3. Ask AR team cho advertiser list

TYPICAL ADVERTISER PROFILES:

TYPE A: "Full Stack" Advertiser
├── Own products + Call center + Warehouse + Fulfillment
├── Ví dụ: Everad (khi họ advertise trên network khác)
├── Pros: reliable, full control
├── Cons: có thể trở thành competitor
└── Thường là partner tốt nhất

TYPE B: "Product + CC" Advertiser
├── Own products + Call center, outsource fulfillment
├── Pros: good at conversion (own CC)
├── Cons: delivery issues (3rd party logistics)
└── Cần monitor delivery SLA

TYPE C: "Product Only" Advertiser
├── Only own product, outsource CC + fulfillment
├── Pros: can focus on product quality
├── Cons: less control over conversion flow
└── Higher risk of issues

TYPE D: "White Label" Advertiser
├── Don't even make product (OEM/white-label)
├── Pros: can pivot fast, many offers
├── Cons: product quality variable
└── Need careful vetting
```

### Major Traffic Sources & Their Affiliates

```

├── Largest traffic source cho nutra
├── Top affiliates: teams of 10-50 people
├── Ad spend: $10K-500K/month per team

├── Trend: getting harder, AI detection improving

TIKTOK
├── Fastest growing for nutra (especially Asia)

├── Lower CPM than Facebook in many GEOs

├── MGID (https://www.mgid.com) — #1 for CIS nutra
├── Taboola (https://www.taboola.com) — global reach

├── Trend: stable, good for evergreen offers

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
├── PropellerAds (https://propellerads.com)
├── RichPush (https://richpush.co)
├── Zeropark (https://zeropark.com)

SEO / ORGANIC
```

### What to look for khi spy

```
WEEKLY SPY ROUTINE (30 phút/tuần):

1. OFFER TRENDS (15 min)
├── Search top offer categories trên AdSpy
├── Filter: last 7 days, target GEOs
├── Note: new products appearing, old ones disappearing
├── Track: which offers are scaling (more creatives = scaling)
└── Output: "Hot offers this week" list

2. CREATIVE TRENDS (10 min)
├── What ad formats performing? (Video vs Image vs Carousel)
├── What angles? (Fear, Aspiration, Testimonial, News-style)
├── What hooks? (First 3 seconds of video)
├── Landing page styles: long-form vs short, video LP vs text
└── Output: Creative trend notes for AM team to share with affiliates

3. COMPETITOR PAYOUT CHECK (5 min)
├── Check Dr.Cash, Everad, LeadBit public offer walls
├── Compare their payout vs ours for same/similar offers
├── Flag if competitor payout > ours + 10%
└── Output: Payout competitiveness report
```
